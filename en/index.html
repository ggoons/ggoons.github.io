Introduction

How can I use

You can use ggoons coding service with system design service or without system design service.

With system design service

First of all, you should make document about what you want a to develop. There is no rule for this document. Just describe the desired system freely. After that, send this document to ggoon@ggoons.com with how much is your budget to this project. GGoons will read this email and document as soon as possible. After that, ggoons will reply this email to you to negotiate development system volumn and final prices.


Without system design service

In this case, you should send system design files such as database schema and specifications for each pages in development system. So, please send system design files with how much is expected or fixed your budget to ggoon@ggoons.com. GGoons will read this email and system design files as soon as possible. After that, ggoons will reply this email to you to negotiate final prices.


Contract fee

After make contract with ggoons to use ggoons services, You should deposit 10% of total development fee as contract fee to ggoons account. GGoons will start work after confirm contract fee is deposited. So, please aware that after ggoons start work, the down payment deposited by the user will not be returned.

Attention please

Your request will be rejected if your budget is too low to develop your system.
